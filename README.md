## üìà Insights & Findings  

Based on the dashboard analysis:

- **Units sold** increase sharply toward the end of the year, with **October** showing the highest spike.  
- **Profit by month** also peaks in **October**, making it the most successful period overall.  
- **Malaysia** contributes the largest portion of total profit across all markets.  
- **Chocolate Chip**, **White Chocolate Macadamia Nut**, and **Snickerdoodle** are the **top profit-generating cookie types**.  
- Monthly trends show a **mid-year dip**, followed by strong growth from **September to December**.
